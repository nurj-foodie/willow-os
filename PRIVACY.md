Welcome to Willow OS. Your privacy is a fundamental part of our "Flow, don't force" philosophy.

## 1. Information We Collect
To provide a synchronized experience across your devices, we collect:
- **Identifier:** Your email address (used for secure Magic Link and Passkey login).
- **User Content:** Your tasks, priorities, and moods, stored securely in our cloud database.
- **Financial Data:** If you use the Willow Ledger, we store transaction amounts and categories you manually enter.

## 2. Data Protection (PDPA & GDPR)
Willow OS is designed to be lean and private:
- **Malaysia PDPA:** In accordance with the Personal Data Protection Act 2010, we ensure your personal data is processed only for its intended purpose.
- **Cloud Storage:** We use Supabase (built on AWS/Google Cloud) with Singapore/Global regions to ensure high security and low latency.
- **Encryption:** All data is encrypted in transit and at rest.

## 3. Your Rights
You own your data. You can:
- **Access:** View all your data within the app at any time.
- **Delete:** You can request account deletion, which will permanently remove all your tasks and credentials from our servers.

## 4. Third-Party Services
We do not sell your data. We only use trusted infrastructure providers:
- **Supabase:** Database and Authentication.
- **Cloudflare:** Website hosting and security.

## 5. Contact
For privacy concerns, reach out to your local Willow instance administrator.
